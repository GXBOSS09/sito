General rules

    The world is beautiful because it is diverse, and so is the virtual world of example.

    That's why we're committed to ensuring that everyone can participate in our community safely.

    To that end, we've introduced some rules and guidelines that everyone needs to follow, regarding posts, comments, and usernames.

    Check out the Minecraft and Discord server policies below.

Discord server

  1. Images, videos and more that favor dangerous driving are strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to: using your mobile phone while driving on public roads if it is considered unsafe, driving over the speed limit on public roads, showing modifications and/or illegal/dangerous/unsuitable vehicles. Dashcam videos are considered exceptions, as long as you have not caused damage yourself.

  4. Share personal information about you and others (es. name, address, email, phone number, etc.).
  5. ASCII art and the like, with the exception of the /shrug and /tableflip commands.
  6. Discussions leading to agitation, including political discussions.
  7. Repeat a bot command that was just sent by another user.
  8. Ask others to buy or give you things.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
